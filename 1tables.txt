Tablas
******

Users: id, first_name, last_name, email, role, password(hashed), course_id(relacion*)
  -Roles: user(default), admin, teacher 

Courses: id, course_name, description, modality, duration, price, active, start_date, finish_date, type, teacher(relacion*)
  -modality: presencial/distancia
  -duration: semanas
  -type: carrera/capacitacion/etc.

Teachers: id, first_name, last_name 

Queries: id, name, email, reason, description, date

*Relaciones en los junction models


1.Tabla "Users":

-id (primary key)
-first_name
-last_name
-email
-role (user-default-/admin/teacher)
-password (hashed)
*-course_id (foreign key relacionada a courses)

2.Tabla "Courses":
-id (primary key)
-course_name
-description
-modality (presencial/distancia)
-duration (semanas)
-price
-active
-start_date
-finish_date
-type (carrera/capacitacion/etc.)
*-teacher (foreign key relacionada a teachers)

3.Tabla "Teachers":
-id (primary key)
-first_name
-last_name

4.Tabla "Queries":
-id (primary key)
-name
-email
-reason
-description
-date
